### Be Authentic
- Share genuine moments from your life

### Be Supportive
- Celebrate your friends' wins

---

## What's Not Allowed

### Harassment & Bullying
- No targeted harassment of any user
- No threats, intimidation, or stalking
- No encouraging others to harass someone
- No sharing private information without consent (doxxing)

### Hate Speech
- No content promoting hatred based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics
- No slurs or dehumanizing language
- No symbols or imagery associated with hate groups

### Violence
- No threats of violence
- No content glorifying violence
- No content promoting self-harm or suicide
- If you're struggling, please reach out to a crisis helpline

### Sexual Content
- No nudity or sexually explicit content
- No sexual solicitation
- No content sexualizing minors (zero tolerance)

### Illegal Activity
- No content promoting illegal activities
- No drug sales or solicitation
- No fraud or scams
- No weapons sales

### Spam & Manipulation
- No spam or repetitive content
- No fake accounts or impersonation
- No coordinated inauthentic behavior
- No bots or automated posting

### Misinformation
- No deliberately false information that could cause harm
- No conspiracy theories that promote violence
- No health misinformation

### Intellectual Property
- No posting content you don't have rights to
- Respect copyrights and trademarks
- Give credit where it's due

## Group Chat Guidelines (Besties & Squad)

### Besties (4 people)
Your closest circle. These are the people you trust completely.
- Keep sensitive conversations here
- Respect the intimate nature of the group
- What's shared in Besties stays in Besties

### Squad (12 people)
Your inner circle. Close friends you see regularly.
- Be inclusive in conversations
- Don't exclude members from important discussions
- Keep it supportive

### All Groups
- Respect the group's purpose
- Don't add people without group consensus
- Handle conflicts privately when possible

## Reporting & Enforcement

### How to Report
1. Tap the three dots on any post or message
2. Select "Report"
3. Choose the reason
4. Add any additional context

### What Happens Next
- We review all reports within 24 hours
- We may contact you for more information
- We'll take action if guidelines are violated

### Enforcement Actions

| Violation Level | Action |
|-----------------|--------|
| Minor/First offense | Warning |
| Repeated violations | Temporary suspension (1-7 days) |
| Serious violations | Account suspension |
| Severe violations | Permanent ban |

We consider:
- Severity of the violation
- Intent
- History of violations
- Impact on others

### Appeals
If you believe we made an error:
1. Email appeals@real-ones.app
2. Include your username and explanation
3. We'll review within 7 days
