# Data Deletion Policy

**REALones**  
**Effective Date: January 17, 2025**

---

## Your Right to Delete Your Data

At REALones, we believe you should have full control over your data. You can request deletion of your account and personal data at any time.

---

## How to Delete Your Account

### Option 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

1. Open the REALones app
2. Go to **Profile** → **Settings**
3. Tap **Account**
4. Tap **Delete Account**
5. Confirm by entering your password or using biometric authentication
6. Tap **Permanently Delete**

### Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to **privacy@real-ones.app** with:
- Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
- Your registered email address
- Your username (if known)

We will process your request within 7 business days.

---

## What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

| Data Type | Deleted |
|-----------|---------|
| Profile information (name, email, photo) | ✅ Yes |
| Posts you created | ✅ Yes |
| Direct messages you sent | ✅ Yes |
| Group messages you sent | ✅ Yes |
| Friend connections | ✅ Yes |
| Push notification tokens | ✅ Yes |
| In-app purchase history | ❌ No* |
| Anonymous analytics data | ❌ No** |

*In-app purchase records are maintained by Apple and required for refund processing.

**Anonymous, aggregated analytics data that cannot identify you may be retained.

---

## Deletion Timeline

| Stage | Timeframe |
|-------|-----------|
| Account deactivation | Immediate |
| Data removed from active systems | Within 24 hours |
| Data removed from backups | Within 30 days |
| Confirmation email sent | Within 7 days |

## Before You Delete

### Consider These Alternatives

**Taking a Break?**
- You can simply log out and return anytime
- Your account will remain inactive but preserved

**Issues with the App?**
- Contact support@real-ones.app
- We'd love to help resolve any problems

**Privacy Concerns?**
- Review our Privacy Policy
- Adjust your settings for more privacy
- Contact us with specific concerns

### What You'll Lose

- All your posts and content
- Your friend connections
- Message history
- Your username (may not be reclaimable)
- Any purchased friend slots

### What You Can Export First

Before deleting, you can request a copy of your data:
1. Email privacy@real-ones.app
2. Subject: "Data Export Request"
3. We'll send you a file within 7 days containing:
   - Your profile information
   - Your posts
   - Your friend list
   - Your messages

---

## Third-Party Data

Some data may exist with third-party services:

| Service | Data | How to Delete |
|---------|------|---------------|
| Apple | Sign-in credentials | Settings → Apple ID → Apps Using Apple ID |
| Google | Sign-in credentials | myaccount.google.com → Security → Third-party apps |
| Facebook | Imported friends | Data is only stored locally during import |

Deleting your REALones account does not delete data held by these third parties.

---

## Messages to Others

When you delete your account:

- **Your messages in DMs**: Deleted from your side; recipient may still see them
- **Your messages in groups**: Deleted and replaced with "Message deleted"
- **Others' messages to you**: No longer accessible by you

---

## Reactivation

### Within 30 Days
If you change your mind within 30 days:
- Email support@real-ones.app
- We may be able to restore your account

### After 30 Days
- Your data is permanently deleted
- You cannot recover your account
- You can create a new account with the same email

## GDPR & CCPA Rights

If you're in the EU (GDPR) or California (CCPA), you have additional rights:

- **Right to be forgotten** - Covered by this policy
- **Right to data portability** - Request export before deletion
- **Right to object** - Contact us with concerns

We respond to all verified requests within 30 days.
