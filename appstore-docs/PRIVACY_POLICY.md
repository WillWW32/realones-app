**REALones**

## Introduction

REALones ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

**Account Information**
- Name
- Email address
- Profile photo (optional)
- Authentication credentials (via Apple Sign In or Google Sign In)

**User Content**
- Posts and messages you create
- Friends list and connections
- Group memberships (Besties, Squad)

**Imported Data**
- Facebook friends list (only when you choose to import)
- Contact information you choose to share

### Information Collected Automatically

**Device Information**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- Push notification tokens

**Usage Information**
- App features used
- Time spent in app
- Interaction patterns

**Log Data**
- IP address
- Access times
- Error logs

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide and maintain the App** - Create your account, enable connections with friends, and deliver our services

2. **Facilitate communication** - Enable messaging between you and your friends, send notifications about messages and friend requests

3. **Improve the App** - Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and user experience

4. **Send notifications** - Deliver push notifications about new messages, friend requests, and app updates (with your permission)

5. **Ensure security** - Protect against unauthorized access, fraud, and abuse

6. **Comply with legal obligations** - Meet applicable laws and regulations

---

## How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal information.

We may share your information in these limited circumstances:

**With Other Users**
- Your profile name and photo are visible to your friends
- Your posts are visible to your friends
- Messages are visible to conversation participants

**Service Providers**
- Cloud hosting (Supabase)
- Authentication services (Apple, Google)
- Push notification services (Expo)

**Legal Requirements**
- When required by law
- To protect our rights or safety
- To investigate potential violations

**Business Transfers**
- In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

---

## Data Storage and Security

**Storage Location**  
Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Supabase, located in the United States.

**Security Measures**
- Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Encryption at rest
- Secure authentication
- Regular security audits
- Access controls

**Retention**  
We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time, which will remove your data from our systems within 30 days.

---

## Your Rights and Choices

**Access and Portability**  
You can access your data through the App settings or by contacting us.

**Correction**  
You can update your profile information at any time in the App.

**Deletion**  
You can delete your account in the App settings. This will permanently remove:
- Your profile and account information
- Your posts and messages
- Your friend connections

**Push Notifications**  
You can disable push notifications in your device settings.

**Data Export**  
You can request a copy of your data by contacting us at privacy@real-ones.app.

## Children's Privacy

REALones is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete that information promptly.

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

- Restrict processing

- Object to processing
- Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

**Legal Basis for Processing:**
- Consent (for optional features)

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@real-ones.app.
